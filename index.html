Instructions

How to play Ping Pong.
  1. The Ai modal will identify the location of your wrist, Please keep it in the frame for it to identify
  2. Your wrist should be 3-4 ft away from your camera
  3. There should be a red dot showing up on the Web camera located on your right wrist
  4. Move your RIGHT wrist up and down to move your ping pong paddle (red paddle)
  5. AND MOST IMPORTANTLY HAVE FUN!!!
